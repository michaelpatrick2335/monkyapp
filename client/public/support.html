Quick answers

How do I cancel my subscription?

iPhone users: Open the Settings app on your iPhone → tap your name at the top → tap Subscriptions → tap Monky → tap Cancel Subscription.

Web users: Sign in at monkyapp.com, go to Settings, and click Manage Subscription.

How do I request a refund?

iOS purchases: Refunds for App Store purchases are handled by Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com and select your Monky purchase to request a refund.

Web purchases: Email support@monkyapp.com within 14 days of purchase and we'll review your request.

I can't sign in / I forgot my password.

Go to monkyapp.com/app and tap "Forgot password." We'll send a reset link to your email. If you don't see it, check your spam folder. Still stuck? Email support@monkyapp.com.

The app crashed or isn't working right.

Try these steps in order: (1) force-quit and reopen the app, (2) restart your phone, (3) check that you're on the latest version in the App Store. If the issue persists, email support@monkyapp.com with your device model, iOS version, and a short description — bonus points for a screenshot.

How do I delete my account?

Open the app → Settings → Delete Account. Or email support@monkyapp.com from your account email and we'll handle it within 7 business days. All your data, including journal entries, will be permanently removed within 30 days.

How do I export my journal entries?

Open the app → Settings → Export Data. We'll email you a JSON file of your journal entries within a few minutes. This feature is free for all users, paid or not.

I signed up on the web. Can I use the iPhone app?

Yes. Sign in to the iPhone app with the same email and password — your journal, streaks, and progress sync automatically. Note: subscriptions started on the web ($4.99/mo) are managed through Stripe; subscriptions started on iOS ($6.99/mo) are managed through Apple. They are separate, so don't subscribe in both places.

What's the difference between web and iOS pricing?

Monky Premium is $4.99/month on the web (paid via Stripe) and $6.99/month on iOS (paid via Apple). The price difference covers Apple's App Store fee. Features and content are identical on both platforms.
